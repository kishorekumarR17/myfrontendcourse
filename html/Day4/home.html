TN ELECTION RESULT 2026

The 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election produced a major political change in the state. Vijay’s party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK), emerged as the single largest party by winning 108 seats out of 234. The DMK won 59 seats, while AIADMK secured 47 seats. Other parties like Congress, PMK, and IUML won a smaller number of seats. Since the majority mark is 118 seats, TVK initially fell short of forming the government on its own. Later, support from Congress and some alliance parties reportedly helped TVK cross the majority mark. This election is considered historic because it broke the long-standing dominance of DMK and AIADMK in Tamil Nadu politics. Reports also suggest that Vijay may soon take oath as the new Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu for more details.
