Introduction

Web development is the process of creating websites and web applications. It helps people access information, communicate online, shop, learn, and use different services through the internet. Web development is an important part of modern technology.

Frontend Development

Frontend development focuses on the visible part of a website that users interact with. It uses technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to design webpages, add colors, create layouts, and make websites interactive and user-friendly.

Backend Development

Backend development manages the server, database, and application logic of a website. It works behind the scenes to store data, process requests, and provide security. Languages like Python, Java, PHP, and Node.js are commonly used for backend development.

Importance of Web Development

Web development is important for businesses, education, banking, entertainment, and communication. Websites help companies promote their services, students learn online, and users connect with people around the world. It also creates many job opportunities in the IT industry.

Conclusion

Web development plays a major role in the digital world. It combines creativity and technology to build useful websites and applications. With the growth of the internet, web development continues to become more important in everyday life.
